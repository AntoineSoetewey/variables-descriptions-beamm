\textbf{Short Description:} Number of months spent in part-time work as
self-employed {[}including family worker{]}

\textbf{Long Description:} Covers months where the respondent worked
part-time as a self-employed or family worker. Respondents select the
activity category based on the role occupying most of their time.
Excludes unpaid or voluntary work, with special codes for seasonal or
variable workers【141†Personal data (P-file)-75-209.pdf】.
